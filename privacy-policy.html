Privacy Policy for The Hunter

Effective Date: May 19, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to "The Hunter" ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you play our game within the Pi Network ecosystem.

2. Information We Collect

To provide a seamless gaming experience, we only access user data made available through the Pi Network SDK:

  • Pi Network Username / Wallet Address: To identify your account, save your high scores, game progress, and process in-game transactions.
  • We DO NOT collect, store, or share any personal identifiable information (PII) such as your real name, phone number, email address, or geographic location.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected Pi Network identifiers solely to:

  • Authenticate your login via Pi SDK.
  • Maintain your in-game assets, inventory, and hunting progress.
  • Facilitate secure in-game Pi transactions.

4. Data Security

We implement strict industry-standard security measures to protect your game data. Since authentication is handled natively by the Pi Network SDK, we never see or store your Pi Wallet passphrase or private keys.

5. Third-Party Services

Our game operates exclusively within the Pi Browser. We do not sell, trade, or rent your blockchain identifiers to third parties.

6. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be updated directly within the app configuration.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our official developer channels in the Pi Network.
